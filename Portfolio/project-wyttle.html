What it does

Wyttle is a cross-platform mobile app designed to connect peers-to-peers and peers-to-mentors. It supports discovery, matching, secure communication, and token-based interactions to encourage engagement.

Key features

  • Role-based experience (mentor vs mentee)
  • Discovery and matching flows
  • Messaging with notifications
  • Token-based incentives and feedback loops
